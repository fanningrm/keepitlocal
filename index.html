Introduction

Fighting Back

Cities and towns throughout the country are joining forces with institutions of higher learning like Purdue University, state chambers of commerce, and local chapters of the American Water Works Association to oppose the special interests.

At stake are our vital public water works projects, the sustainability and durability of our critical water infrastructures, and the knowledge and expertise of the water professionals we rely on for safe, clean and reliable drinking water available at the turn of a tap.
